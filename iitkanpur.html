IITK FOUNDATION FOR ADVANCED CONTINUING EDUCATION & TRAINING (IFACET)

IITK Foundation for Continuing Education & Training (IFACET) is a company incorporated by Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur

  • To administer, operate and manage all activities related to continuing education for students, researchers, faculty, industry professional, institutions, industry and other individuals.

  • To provide education training and augmented learning in all domains of engineering, sciences, humanities, medical sciences, economics, management and arts by various value- added short- and long- term certification programs, seminars and conferences, globally using online, offline, cyber physical & blended mode of delivery.

  • To provide training and consultancy services to the industries, associations and institutions.

IFACET ACTIVITIES




IFACET delivers through its partners Self-paced, Online Live, Blended and Classroom training courses in emerging technologies, engineering, arts and management education.


All programmes come with Block Chain digital certifications from EICTA - IIT Kanpur |



ABOUT IFACT
